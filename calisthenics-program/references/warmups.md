# Warmup Templates by Session Type

## General Calisthenics Warmup (5–8 min)
Good for any session. Gets joints mobile, core primed, CNS activated.

1. Arm circles — 10 forward, 10 backward
2. Hip circles — 10 each direction
3. World's Greatest Stretch — 5 each side
4. Cat-Cow — 10 reps
5. Deep Squat hold — 30 sec
6. Shoulder dislocates (band or stick) — 10
7. Dead Hang — 20–30 sec
8. Scapular push-ups — 10
9. Scapular pull-ups — 10
10. Inchworm — 5 reps

---

## Upper Body / Push Session Warmup (5–7 min)
Focus: Shoulders, wrists, thoracic spine

1. Wrist circles — 30 sec each direction
2. Wrist push-ups (on knuckles) — 10
3. Wrist extensions (passive stretch on floor) — 30 sec
4. Shoulder dislocates — 10–15
5. Band pull-aparts (or hug-and-extend) — 15
6. Wall slides (arms slide up wall, maintain contact) — 10
7. Push-up to downward dog — 8 reps
8. Pike push-ups (light, slow) — 8 reps

---

## Pull Session Warmup (5–7 min)
Focus: Shoulders, lats, biceps, grip

1. Dead hang — 30 sec
2. Shoulder circles — 10 each direction
3. Band pull-aparts — 15
4. Scapular pull-ups — 10
5. Cat-Cow — 10
6. Thoracic extension over foam roller — 30 sec
7. Supinated hang (underhand grip) — 20 sec
8. Australian rows (light, full ROM) — 8

---

## Leg Session Warmup (5–8 min)
Focus: Hips, ankles, glutes, hamstrings

1. Hip circles — 10 each direction
2. Leg swings (front-back) — 10 each leg
3. Leg swings (side-to-side) — 10 each leg
4. Cossack squats — 5 each side
5. World's Greatest Stretch — 5 each side
6. Ankle circles — 10 each direction
7. Glute bridges — 15
8. Air squats (controlled, full depth) — 10
9. Jump squats (light) — 5

---

## Skill Session Warmup: Handstand (10 min)
This is a full wrist + shoulder prep specific to handstand practice.

1. Wrist circles — 1 min
2. Wrist push-ups on fists — 10
3. Wrist extension stretch (on floor) — 45 sec
4. Wrist flexion stretch (fingers back) — 45 sec
5. Shoulder dislocates — 15
6. Wall slides — 10
7. Cat-Cow — 10
8. Crow pose / frog stand — 20 sec
9. Plank shoulder taps — 10 each
10. Pike push-ups — 8
11. Wall handstand hold (light, shoulders open) — 1–2 × 15 sec

---

## Skill Session Warmup: Rings / Muscle-Up (8 min)
1. Arm circles — 20
2. Dead hang (straight arm) — 30 sec
3. Scapular pull-ups — 10
4. Band pull-aparts — 15
5. Ring support hold (feet on floor) — 20 sec
6. Ring dips (partial) — 8
7. High pull-ups (chest-to-bar if possible) — 5
8. Shoulder dislocates — 10

---

## Cool-down / Mobility Templates (5 min)

### Post-Push Cool-down
- Doorway chest stretch — 30 sec each angle
- Child's pose with arm reach — 45 sec
- Lat stretch (overhead, leaning into doorframe) — 30 sec each
- Wrist flexor stretch — 30 sec

### Post-Pull Cool-down
- Cross-body shoulder stretch — 30 sec each
- Bicep stretch (arm back, palm up on wall) — 30 sec each
- Thread-the-needle thoracic stretch — 30 sec each
- Dead hang (passive, relaxed) — 30 sec

### Post-Leg Cool-down
- Figure-4 glute stretch — 45 sec each
- Standing quad stretch — 30 sec each
- Hip flexor lunge stretch — 45 sec each
- Seated hamstring stretch — 45 sec
- Calf stretch on step — 30 sec each

### Post-Skill (Handstand) Cool-down
- Child's pose — 45 sec
- Thread-the-needle — 30 sec each side
- Wrist flexor + extensor stretch — 30 sec each
- Shoulder cross-body stretch — 30 sec each
